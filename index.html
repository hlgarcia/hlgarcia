A Failed Policy

The Philippine Drug War is also known as the “War on Drugs” it is the drug policy followed by the Philippine government implemented by our President Rodrigo Duterte. The drug policy's goal is to neutralize illegal personalities nationwide. However, instead of being admitted to drug addiction therapy, people are being murdered for using or selling drugs. Police, almost daily, kill unarmed people whom they believe are suspects, and then they proceed to place guns or drugs or other illicit items at the crime to make it seem real. Ever since this policy was implemented, more than 12,000 Filipino citizens have been killed either by police forces or other citizens in extrajudicial killings. The lack of concern for the Filipino society from the government is overly harrowing. This policy is harming families and communities around the Philippines. Hopefully, one day the international community can figure out a way to stop these atrocities from continuing to occur.
